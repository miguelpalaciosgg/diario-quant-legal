Diario Quant uses this application to create and publish short-form videos on authorized social media accounts. This policy explains what data is used, why it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Process

We may process the following data strictly to operate the publishing workflow:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens from connected platforms such as TikTok.
  • Account identifiers and basic creator information returned by platform APIs.
  • Video metadata such as title, caption, publication status, and publication timestamps.
  • Operational logs needed to diagnose failures and verify successful publishing.

2. Purpose of Processing

We use this data only to:

  • Authenticate the authorized account.
  • Publish videos requested by the account owner.
  • Store publication status and execution history.
  • Maintain security, reliability, and error recovery of the automation workflow.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share OAuth tokens with third parties except with infrastructure providers strictly required to operate the service, such as secure cloud hosting and secret management tools.

4. Data Retention

Tokens and operational records are retained only as long as needed to maintain the authorized publishing workflow, comply with legal obligations, or support security and troubleshooting.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect credentials and publication data, including server-side storage, restricted access, and secure secret management where available.

6. User Control

The account owner may revoke access at any time through the relevant platform authorization settings. Once access is revoked, future automated publishing will stop until the account is connected again.

7. Contact

For privacy questions related to this application, contact Diario Quant through the official project contact channel.
